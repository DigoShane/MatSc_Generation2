The ultraslow fluctuations may come from uniform magnetic fluctuations spread around the wave vector q = 0, otherwise the electric origins. The 63 Cu Knight shifts 63 K cc,ab at a magnetic field along the c axis or in the ab plane are the sum of the spin shift K spin and the orbital shift K orb as 63 K cc,ab = K cc,ab spin (T ) + K cc,ab orb . The spin shift is K cc,ab spin (T ) = A hf cc,ab χ cc,ab s (T ) with the hyperfine coupling parameters A hf cc,ab and the uniform spin susceptibility χ cc,ab s (T ). For a temperature-dependent isotropic spin susceptibility χ cc s = χ ab s , the ratio ∆K cc spin /∆K ab spin = (dK cc spin /dT )/(dK ab spin /dT ) is equal to the ratio A hf cc /A hf ab .
