The EMF dependencies for Ag-Au-S systems were analyzed and it was found that the reactions described in reference [1] are inaccurate. Additionally, the assumption that the equilibrium state of Au remains unchanged during the measurement process was shown to be incorrect. Based on the EMF data provided in White's 1957 paper, the compositions of the alloys involved in the reactions were determined. The reaction equations were then modified to include temperature dependencies of the alloy composition.
