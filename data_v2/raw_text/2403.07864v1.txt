In conclusion, we have shown the coherency by 2 signs: 1) X-ray pole figures and 2) a Moiré 
patern by STEM. We have also shown the coherent strain by 3 signs: 1) in-situ RHEED, 2) RSM by

of a dominant coherency and strain at the same �me in qvdWE modes.
