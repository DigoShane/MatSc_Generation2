Conclusion. In the paper the quasi-particle interpretation of the quantization of the energy of crystallite of PC ag- gregate under PD is given. Within this interpretation the quantum of energy of unit dislocation, condition- 10 ally called as the dislocon, appears by the composite (short-lived) particle consisting from the acoustic phonons surrounding two atoms (at the moment of time of breaking the bond between the atoms and cre- ating of a nanopore), which leave the nodes of the crystal lattice under PD. This idea allows one to come analytically to the description and evolution of the Chernov-Luders macroband of PD in connection with observed acoustic emission . The quasi-particle interpretation adds the significant argument in flavor of the concept of the origin of 1D-defects (dislocations) in terms of 0D-defects (nanopore, vacan- cy) under PD process. It was shown within one-phase model of an single-mode PC material that with the grows of the temperature the maximum of the yield strength m increases in such a way that in the coarse-grained limit the yield strength, y  , decreases down to new critical size of the grain d1: 0 1 3 d  d ~ d , whereas for the grains with d  d1 the values of y  , including maximum m , increase as it was theoretically demonstrated for pure Al on the Fig. 2.
